Waste Buddy

  • Apple Core

    Fruits get COMPOSTED!
  • Bones

    Bones get COMPOSTED!
  • Boxes-cardboard

    Cardboard gets RECYCLED!
  • Cans-Soda

    Aluminum cans get RECYCLED!
  • Used Cups

    Whether plastic or paper, disposable cups are to be put in the RECYCLE.
  • Diapers

    Diapers, even ones labeled as compostable, go in TRASH!
  • Eyeglasses, sunglasses

    If they can't be donated, these go in the GARBAGE!
  • Fluorescent Light Bulbs

    SPECIAL DISPOSAL!! DO NOT put in ANY Bin, but take to Hazardous Waste Collection
  • Grease, Oils

    Contain securely and dispose in the TRASH!
  • House Plants

    Without the pot, put into COMPOST!
  • Iron, Steel Metal pieces

    Metal pieces, even rusted ones, go into RECYCLING!
  • Junk Mail

    Mail even with plastic wrap goes into RECYCLING!
  • Kleenex

    Tissue paper gets COMPOSTED!
  • Pizza

    Any unfinished pizza goes in the COMPOST
  • Sandwich

    The tasty sandwich that you couldn't finish. COMPOST
  • Uncoated Paper Plate

    As long as the paper plates aren't waxed(They will have a sheen), they go in the COMPOST
